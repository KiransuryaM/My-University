OUR CAMPUS

The Presidency University functions on a 62-Acre lush green Campus. This State of the Art Campus
is professionally equipped with the latest in Design and Technology. Its Buildings are aesthetically
designed with optimal utilization of the floor space.

Our campus contains following schools

  • School of Engineering
  • School of Law
  • School of Design
  • School of Management
  • School of Science



Our Mission

  • Commit to be an innovative and inclusive institution by seeking excellence in teaching, research and knowledge transfer.

  • Pursue research and development and its dissemination to the community at large.

  • Provide knowledge-based technological support and services to the industry in its growth and development.

  • Create, sustain and apply learning in an interdisciplinary environment with consideration for ethical,
    ecological and economic aspects of nation building.
